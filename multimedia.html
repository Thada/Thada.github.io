Multimedia


Here I created a 3D animation that told a brief narrative. I began with the intention of making a story about how something mundane can become something exciting, much like how a child would create their own adventures using their imagination. After drafting a script and creating a short storyboard, I went on to begin working on everything else. This video was created using Blender for modeling and animating, Audacity for sound editing, and Adobe Photoshop for textures and other 2D elements.
