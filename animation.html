Animation


In this course I was asked to come up with a character that is a parody of some other person or character. Before I began animating, I created several sketches and a storyboard to flesh out the plot. This video was created using a combination of Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, and Illustrator, as well as Audacity for adding and editing sounds.
